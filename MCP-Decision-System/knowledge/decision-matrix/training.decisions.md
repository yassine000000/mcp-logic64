# Training Decision Matrix

| Condition | Insight | Allowed Decision | Exposure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Fatigue > 80% | "User is exhausted" | Suggest Rest Day | Auto-Pilot |
| HR Spike > Max | "Abnormal Heart Rate" | Abort Session & Alert | High-Priority Notification |
| Performance < 50% | "Underperforming" | Lower Intensity by 10% | Auto-Pilot |
| User Reports Pain | "Potential Injury" | STOP & Refer to Specialist | Human-Review |
